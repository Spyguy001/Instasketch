About Us

We provide an innovative web service and to facilitate primary/elementary school teachers and students by creating an environment to submit and access drawings.
If you heard about us through other schools, that's cool. If you want to experience our service, please sign up and enjoy your drawing classes :)


Students can:
  • Create their own drawings
  • Submit the drawings to the teacher for assessment
  • Send the drawings to their parents
  • View their own profile data and analytics

Teachers can:

  • Create user accounts for students
  • Adjust settings for the teaching session (e.g. name, students involved)
  • Send instructions to the class
  • Retrieve drawings that students have submitted
  • Write comments on students’ drawings
  • Share drawings with the rest of the class
  • View and make changes to any student’s account settings
  • View and make changes to their own account settings
  • View profile analytics for themselves and all students
